About Us

Our Story

TechTuneTrends is our e-commerce website founded by the four collaboration of different online shopping company: TechZone, TromboneHaven, JB's Store, and Maki's Apparel. We collaborate to each other to build an big e-commerce website platform to fulfill our suffecient goal to provide better shopping services to our beloved customers. We started on August 14, 2024 to build our small e-commerce project and now we're successfully reach the scope of serving not only in the Philippines but internationally.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide good online shopping services and provide full satisfaction of our customers. We aim to give you an opportunity to join on trends such as in fashion style, emerging technologies and gadgets, and be in touch with music. Our company aims to be at the top Online Shopping website in the world in the near future.
